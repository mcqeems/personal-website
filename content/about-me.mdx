#### My Journey Into Tech

My path to becoming a developer wasn't a straight line, but it was fueled by a persistent curiosity for how things work. It started with an online game called AQW which was one of the most popular online browser game when i was a kid. This initial spark led me to pursue a degree in **Computer Science** at **University of Darussalam Gontor**, where I began to build a solid foundation in computer science principles.

During my studies, I had the opportunity to dive deep into both front-end and back-end development. I honed my skills as a **Web Developer**. where I worked on projects like **Renaissance**, which become a huge leap point for me because it teaches me alot of things. A pivotal moment was my time as a **PPTIK Staff** for Network Engineering, where I not only solidified my own understanding but also discovered a passion for helping others grasp complex technical concepts.

#### My Philosophy as a Developer

I believe that the best applications are born from a blend of meticulous engineering and a user-centric mindset. My commitment is to not just write code, but to build solutions. For me, that means:

- **Writing Clean & Scalable Code:** I strive to create code that is not only functional today but is also maintainable and ready for future growth.
- **Embracing Challenges:** I see every complex problem as an opportunity to learn and innovate. Whether it's mastering a new framework or architecting a robust system, I'm committed to finding the right solution.
- **Continuous Learning:** The tech world moves fast, and I'm dedicated to moving with it. I'm always exploring new technologies and methodologies to refine my craft.
